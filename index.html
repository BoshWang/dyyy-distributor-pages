DYYY — Movie English

An automated video distribution tool for educational English-learning content.

What is DYYY?

DYYY (电影英语) is an internal tool that publishes our originally produced short videos to creator-owned social media accounts. Each video teaches English vocabulary and idioms through real movie clips, with bilingual subtitles in Chinese, Spanish, Portuguese, Hindi, Japanese, and other languages.

The tool is operated by an individual developer and is used solely to distribute our own content to our own connected accounts on platforms including YouTube, TikTok, Bilibili, and Xiaohongshu.

Features

  • Automated upload of educational short videos to TikTok via the official Content Posting API
  • Multilingual title, description, and tag generation
  • Cross-platform scheduling and status tracking
  • OAuth-based authorization — never stores user passwords
